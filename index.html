monster kitchen and bar of Madrid

Monster kitchen and bar wanders through our ground floor preoccupied with local and seasonal produce prepared for sharing. The kitchen and bar team gedgdsgsdguides her through her multiple personalities as she shift from lucidity to debauchery between 6.30AM until late every day. She is open to everyone for all occasions at all times with a menu that changes with the seasons. She spreads across four separate spaces. The lounge is a series of nested spaces made of woven rough formed concrete structural lintels. The Mosaic room houses giant original artworks. The Salon and Dining rooms tell a story of Australian immigration
HOURS
6.30am – 12.00am every day
